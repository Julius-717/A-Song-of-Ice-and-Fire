What's this all about?

This webapp intends to help those people who haven't read A Song Of Ice And Fire book series to follow the TV series episodes without getting lost in a sea of characters.
The history is packed of names, complex family trees, grudges between characters based on 'historical' events, and a whole world that can be overwhelming for those who didn't abserve all the history through the books before watching the show.
So if you keep this page in your laptop while you watch it, you won't have to ask "who is that Snow guy again?" anymore!
